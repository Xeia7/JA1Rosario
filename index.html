Our Story

A Christ-Centered Family in Rosario

Jesus the Anointed One Church (JA1) Rosario is dedicated to the transformative power of the Gospel. Our heart is to see every individual set free and empowered to live out their divine purpose within a supportive community.

Spiritual Growth

Deepening our relationship with God through Word and Worship.

Stronger Families

Building foundations that last for generations.

Our Vision

"To be a global church, empowering people of all walks of life, through the preaching of the Gospel of Jesus Christ, setting the captives free."

Our Mission

  • Equipping believers for the work of the ministry.
  • Reaching out to the lost in Rosario and beyond.
  • Fostering a culture of love, holiness, and power.
